OUR SERVICES

CARGO TRANSPORTATION BY RAILWAY

We offer the import service of vegetables and sugar, as well as the service of gas import by rail.

LOGISTIC SERVICES

Offering large number of logistics management services and solutions according to customer requirements.

IMPORT & EXPORT

We offer import and export customs clearance services, with offices in the main ports and Mexican borders.

GROUND TRANSPORTATION

We have alliances with local transport companies with platforms, refrigerated boxes, dry boxes and a large number of units at our disposition for the provision of transportation services for your products.

CUSTOM-MADE DUTY

We determine what kind of duty is adjusted to your merchandise for the correct fulfillment of non-tariff restrictions and regulations as well as fair payment of taxes and foreign trade rights.

CUSTOM CONSULTING

We teach to our clients how to register in the importers registry as well as the non-tariff regulations and restrictions of foreign trade merchandise, as well as propose, coordinate and supervise the logistic chain appropriate to their needs.
